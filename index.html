Privacy Policy for CM-Ads-Manager

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

At CM-Ads-Manager, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data when using our Facebook Marketing API integration.

1. Information We Collect

We access your name, email address, and Facebook Ad Account data (IDs, campaign stats, and budgets) through Meta's secure OAuth process.

2. How We Use Data

The data is used strictly to provide the dashboard features of CM-Ads-Manager, allowing you to monitor and manage your ads. We do not use this data for any other purpose.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal or advertising data with any third-party companies.

4. Data Retention

We only store your access tokens temporarily. You can revoke our access at any time through your Facebook settings.

5. Contact Information

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: info@thirdeyeinsights.ca

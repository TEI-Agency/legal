Privacy Policy for CM-Ads-Manager

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

At Third Eye Insights, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our application, **CM-Ads-Manager**, integrated with the Meta Marketing API.

1. Information We Collect

Through our integration with the Meta Marketing API, we access the following types of information via secure OAuth tokens:

  • Profile Information: We access your name and email address as provided by your Facebook profile to identify your account.
  • Advertising Data: We access ad account IDs, campaign names, statistics (spend, impressions, clicks), budgets, and creative performance metrics. This is necessary to provide our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information solely to provide and improve the functionality of CM-Ads-Manager. This includes:

  • Displaying your advertising metrics in a unified dashboard.
  • Allowing you to monitor and analyze your ad campaigns directly from our interface.
  • Generating automated insights and reports based on your ad account performance.

We **do not** sell, rent, or trade your personal or advertising data with any third-party companies.

3. Data Retention and Security

We implement technical security measures to protect your data. Your Meta API access tokens are stored securely and encrypted. We only retain data for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide the service. You may revoke our access at any time through your Facebook settings.

4. Data Subject Rights

You have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. Please see our Data Deletion page for instructions on revoking access and requesting data removal.

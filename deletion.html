Data Deletion Instructions

According to Meta's policies for Platform data, we provide a clear path for users to request the deletion of their data and revoke application access. Please follow the instructions below:

Revoking Application Access (Self-Service)

Step 1: Access your Facebook account **Settings & Privacy > Settings**.

Step 2: Look for **Apps and Websites** in the left-hand menu.

Step 3: Find **CM-Ads-Manager** in the list of active apps and click the **Remove** button.

Once removed, the application will no longer have access to your data or ad account metrics.

Requesting Manual Data Deletion

If you have revoked access and wish to request that we manually remove any residual cached information associated with your account from our temporary logs (which are usually cleared within 30 days automatically), please send an email to our compliance team:

Email: info@thirdeyeinsights.ca

Please include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 48 hours.

Data Deletion Instructions

CM-Ads-Manager does not store your personal data on our servers permanently. However, if you wish to remove the application's access to your Facebook account, follow these steps:

Step 1: Go to your Facebook Profile's Settings & Privacy > Settings.
Step 2: Look for Apps and Websites in the left-hand menu.
Step 3: Find CM-Ads-Manager and click Remove.

Manual Data Removal Request

If you would like us to manually delete any cached information related to your account from our logs, please send an email to: info@thirdeyeinsights.ca.

Please include your name and the request "Data Deletion". We will process your request within 48 hours.
